## FIRST CAUSE OF ACTION

### Defamation Per Se

(Libel: private figure-private concern)

14. Plaintiff repeats, realleges and hereby incorporates paragraphs 1 through 13, inclusive above, as if fully set forth herein.

15. Plaintiff is informed and believes, and thereon claims and alleges that defendant harmed Plaintiff by making the following statement: "I state clearly, in the 2 and half years I have known Parish [sic], find him to be a liar, a cheat, self-serving and of low moral character."

16. The statements were made in writing, and transmitted via facsimile, to Davi's Accountant, Child, Van Wagner &amp; Bradshaw &amp; Company, thereby constituting libel.

17. Child, Van Wagner &amp; Bradshaw &amp; Company relayed the defamatory and libelous statements to Davi's current CFO, Margaret Robley, and BookKeeper, Staci Robley, via telephone and by transmitting same in writing.

18. Child, Van Wagner &amp; Bradshaw &amp; Company and Robley reasonably understood that the statements were about Parish Medley, as the statement specifically identified plaintiff (i.e. defamation/libel per se).

19. Child, Van Wagner &amp; Bradshaw &amp; Company and Robley reasonably understood the statements to mean that plaintiff had committed an illegal act, specifically with respect to the Note.
